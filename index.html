About Me

Jalal Nasr Eddine is a Hobbyist Photographer. He takes pictures of nature. Born and grew up in Lebanon. He travels to Europe often. Lebanon based photographer, Jalal, has been honing his skills in landscape photography for the past several years. Originally a wedding and event photographer for seven years plus. His goal is to show the beautiful nature of mother Earth. And focus on landscapes, cityscapes, and chasing fabulous sunsets.
